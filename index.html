The Challenge

Philips operates at a scale
that demands smarter data

100+ markets. Multiple business units. A D2C push in Personal Health. Oracle Eloqua, Salesforce Marketing Cloud, and AWS — all generating customer data that rarely talks to each other.

🧩
Fragmented Customer Profiles
Customer data is siloed across Eloqua, SFMC, AWS, and regional CRMs. A Philips customer buying a Sonicare in Germany looks entirely different from the same person purchasing an Airfryer in Singapore.
🌍
100+ Market Complexity
Consent regulations differ radically across markets — GDPR, PDPA, LGPD. Without a unified consent layer, activating audiences globally is slow, risky, and manual.
📉
Wasted Media Spend
Without a real-time identity graph, Philips can't suppress existing customers from acquisition campaigns — leading to significant wasted paid media budget and a poor customer experience.
🔄
D2C Transition Pressure
The shift to D2C in Personal Health requires building direct relationships at scale — impossible without a first-party data strategy and real-time activation capability.
