The Platform

One CDP. Three superpowers.

Zeotap CDP sits between your sources and your activation channels — unifying, governing, and amplifying every customer signal.

Deterministic Identity Resolution

Match customers across Eloqua, Salesforce MC, AWS, and offline data using email, phone, and device IDs — even without third-party cookies.

Cross-Device Graph

Link the same Philips customer across their app, web, connected device, and in-store touchpoints into one persistent profile.

Real-Time Ingestion

Streaming and batch connectors for every source — no ETL bottlenecks, no stale data in your activation stack.

100+ Market Scale

Multi-region architecture with data residency controls built in. One CDP instance, every market, locally compliant.

Consent Orchestration

Capture, store, and propagate consent signals from every touchpoint. Activation is automatically gated by current consent state.

GDPR / CCPA / PDPA Ready

Purpose-based data usage, right-to-erasure workflows, and audit trails built into every profile — not bolted on after.

Data Residency Controls

EU, US, and APAC processing regions configurable per market. No data crosses borders without explicit governance rules.

ISO 27001 & SOC 2 Certified

Enterprise-grade security posture. Your customer data is protected to the highest standards.

Native Eloqua & Salesforce MC Connectors

Push enriched, consented audiences directly into your existing martech — no middleware, no data duplication.

AWS Clean Room Ready

Privacy-safe data collaboration with retail and media partners — without exposing raw customer data.

Paid Media Activation

Suppress existing customers, reach lookalikes, and build suppression lists for Google, Meta, and programmatic — all from one audience builder.

Real-Time Segment Refresh

Audiences update as behaviour changes — not overnight batch jobs. Trigger next-best-action at the moment of intent.
